Fine looking Steak

This fine looking Steak is even too good for Gordon Ramsay.

If you would sell it, it would be at least as expensive as the golden steak Ribery ate.

Ingredients

  • 250g of the finest Steak you can find
  • A lot of Garlic
  • Thyme
  • Butter

Description

  1. Start to heat up your pan until flaming hot.
  2. Put your Steak into the pan with the fat side facing the pan.
  3. After most of the fat is molten, drop the steak into the pan.
  4. Let it sit in the pan for 3min on each side.
  5. After the 3min turn of the heat and add your Butter,Thyme and Garlic.
  6. Cover the pan with tinfoil and put the pan into the oven.
  7. Let it rest there for 6min and your fine.
