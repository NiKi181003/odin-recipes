Lasagna

PewDiePie's favorite Lasagna

This recipe was fully inspired by PewDiePie's hit song Bitch Lasagna.

Ingredients

  • Bechamel Sauce
  • Bolognese Sauce
  • Noodle Plates
  • Cheese

Recipe

  1. Create a base layer of Noodle Plates.
  2. On top of your base layer create a layer of Bolognese Sauce and Bechamel Sauce.
  3. Repeat step 1 and 2 until you have no Sauce left.
  4. Top it of with a final layer of Cheese.
