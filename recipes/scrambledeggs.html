Scrambled Eggs

Golden Scrambled Eggs

Follow this recipe to get the perfectly yellow-golden Scrambled Eggs.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Onions
  • Bacon
  • Your favorite Spices

Description

  1. First crack all of your eggs into a bowl.
  2. Stir them up and add your Spices as much as you want to.
  3. While you complete step 1 and 2 heat up your pan and cut your Bacon and Onions.
  4. Add your Onions into the pan and wait until they're glassy.
  5. Add your Bacon to your Onions.
  6. When your Bacon is crispy brown add your Eggs.
  7. Your Eggs are finished when they're still a bit fluid but most of them are solid.
